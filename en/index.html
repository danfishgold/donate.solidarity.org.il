We are a volunteer-based, aid providing organization which was established at the beginning of the COVID-19 crisis in order to connect volunteers to ones in need of assistance, to provide quick and efficient aid during emergencies, and to increase the solidarity within the Israeli society. Entering the current national lock-down, we have had a rapid increase of Israelis in crisis seeking our aid, and we have prepared accordingly. In order to continue expanding our reach and aid, we need your support.
